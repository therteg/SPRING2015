Aquarius-Aries Compatibility

An Air sign with a Fire sign! It couldn’t have been better because Air keeps the Fire burning and Fire keeps the Air warm! Aquarius is permanently in the Air because (s)he dreams of saving the world, and Aries always burns for the sake of initiatives and action. The unpredictable is what ties them together. Aquarius (ruled by Uranus) is original and imaginative - hence the splendor of the relationship. But Aries (ruled by Mars) is always an enthusiastic who only thinks after (s)he takes action (and not too often though) - hence the “explosive” nature of the couple.

Aquarius and Aries are good for each other because they are active, full of great intentions, thoughts and deeds, sometimes even heroic, and in some cases they are even adventurous.

Aquarius-Gemini Compatibility

Air and Air! What can we expect of them? Maybe just to pay more attention to the sentimental life, not to miss the feelings. The Aquarius-Gemini couple is a special, wonderful, “airy” combination, full of ideas that meet successfully Gemini’s pleasure to pass on the information and Aquarius’ gift to take this information to the highest peaks of mentality.

Gemini says “I’ve got an idea” and Aquarius exclaims “I’ve come up with an idea” - and thus their love takes the shape of mental exercise manifested in conversations, trips, communication, friendly or professional meetings, fast acquisitions of books, newspapers, DVDs or fast solving of telephonic obligations.

Aquarius-Libra Compatibility

The Aquarius-Libra couple will always be comfortable around the others - at parties, shows, interviews, taking part in charity deeds or in partnership negotiations of any kind.This is a couple in which Libra brings refinement and Aquarius brings immensity, in which the heart of Venus (Libra) has the capacity to synchronize its beats with the cerebral rhythms of Uranus (Aquarius).

Isn’t it natural? Isn’t Venus (love) the last, yet perfect creation of Uranus (Space), achieved by a perfect sublimation? This means that love is given by God and this is precisely why it is infinite; we become an ocean of happiness for ourselves and for the others because we experience it.

Aquarius-Sagittarius Compatibility

Talking about this relationship is like reading an encyclopedia of all times: nothing is hidden, unknown or unexplored. Sagittarius’ often prophetic visionariness will always find the necessary support and enthusiasm in Aquarius’ dreams to improve the human condition, and Aquarius’ wish to wander freely through the Realm of Gods will always have a passionate fan in Sagittarius’ passion for exploration.

If they truly love each other, then nobody can affect this sincere, dynamic, often passionate couple, characterized by a real aspiration to break all limits. Don’t think for a moment that there is something that can scare them or make them give up. No way!Sagittarius knows the highest purpose of life very well, the constellation for which his/her heart beats. As for Aquarius, there is no barrier to stop him/her.

Aquarius-Leo Compatibility

“Excellent combination!” most of us would say, because, indeed, good Air can sustain a long Fire. Great commitment (Leo) becomes at any time the support of enthusiasm (Aquarius) that can mean everything.

However, there is some over royalty here: Leo is theatrical and takes, sometimes abusively, the entire scene of Aquarius’ existence, while Aquarius, the king of the whole space by definition, stands no limitation, even if it is out of love.In an Aquarius-Leo couple, each of them tries to impose on the other one what is characteristic of him/herself: Leo - his/her absolute royalism, Aquarius - his/her unlimited freedom.

Aquarius-Aquarius Compatibility

When you see them, don’t be surprised if they’re planning the rehabilitation of the human being at a planetary level and talking about what they could invent to gain more time. Because Uranus’ adventure is, in its essence, an adventure beyond Time, precisely for Space not to be threatened anymore by this “erosion” energy. Because if Uranus loses his Space, (s)he loses the reason of being.

And the heart, also - isn’t it an infinite space? Love - isn’t it a space without which nobody and no one can breathe? Then? Isn’t Time the one that has to be “resolved” into this necessary “limitation”?

Aquarius-Cancer Compatibility

We can assume that Aquarius is too mental and Cancer is too sensitive; we could believe that Aquarius is a born adventurer and Cancer is a true, old-fashioned family man/woman, thus drawing a line between unpredictable freedom and open, often blind conservatism. It’s a relationship that, in a way, can be the expression of the famous generation gap. It’s not always like that. Incompatible signs such as Aquarius and Cancer look for each other when time has come to take the final step towards their spiritual evolution.

Cancer needs to learn to be free and not to depend on anyone, and Aquarius needs to learn to accept love, to let him/herself be overwhelmed, invaded by Cancer’s feelings.

Aquarius-Virgo Compatibility

Yes, a fascination that joins Virgo’s unconditioned commitment and Aquarius’ wild enthusiasm to save the world, which makes them reach different dimensions of life but complementarily, indeed: Aquarius is oriented towards the subtle worlds, looking through the memory of the Universe with his/her imagination, and Virgo towards the material and sensible aspects, revealing the microcosmos that comes into contact with this Universe at the terrestrial level.

Aquarius sacrifices his/her heart for the sake of absolute knowledge, Virgo sacrifices his/her personal life to aunts, uncles, old people and all those that have no support. Aquarius sees the woods and Virgo sees only the trees. What other couple experiences such an inner burning, profoundly purifying for the divine spirit within us? With a Virgo native that purifies the spirit through the body and with an Aquarius that purifies the spirit through the mind, so that divine love manifests plenarily through the human being’s heart!?

Aquarius-Capricorn Compatibility

Interesting combination! But, statistically, it’s quite rare because these two “old” signs don’t seem to need each other too often. Still, if they meet and manage to synchronize their value systems, they can build one of the most spectacular couples.

Capricorn will patiently temper Aquarius’ lack of measure and Aquarius will always suggest new horizons to Capricorn. It is, in fact, the couple that can abolish the generation gap from history, if the Saturnine Capricorn knows what to keep of what is old and if Aquarius knows what to choose of the new values to add to the existing ones. The combination between tradition and innovation can truly be saving sometimes. It’s understandable why, from the winter signs on, love comprises the great problems of humanity. These signs didn’t join the terrestrial existence for themselves, but for the others.

Aquarius-Pisces Compatibility

Yes, this is a couple of free sufferance or of the human kind’s salvation - meaning that each of them has his/her own duality. The Pisces native can choose the direction in this couple if (s)he is allowed to, and Aquarius can decide whether this direction is the necessary one, if (s)he is taken seriously.

In normal or poor social conditions, the Aquarius-Pisces couple can have a peaceful marriage with the subsequent implications. But, at turning points for the humanity, this couple can prove often incredible qualities. And this is exactly because one dreams as much as (s)he can (Aquarius) and the other sacrifices him/herself willingly (Pisces), because they both know, deep inside their heart, that they are here to save the planet.

Aquarius-Taurus Compatibility

Do you, by any chance, doubt it? Do you find it impossible or incredible? Do you think Taurus is too domestic and easy-going, too attached to things and desirous of a peaceful home and high living, and Aquarius too rebel and too “universal”, preoccupied with his/her astral worlds, for them to find a way to get along? If you think so, then let me tell you that you are misled by the shallow aspects of the relationship. And here’s why: In fact, both Taurus and Aquarius are fixed signs, characterized by the storing force - even if Taurus is interested in material possessions and Aquarius in intellectual ones.

Both Taurus and Aquarius are creators by definition - even if Taurus has a real sense of “sensible” aesthetic values while Aquarius an equally real sense of values, but of abstract ones. They both prove a high originality, each of them being a non-conformist in his/her own way, even if Taurus is often classified as conservative.

Aquarius-Scorpio Compatibility

Total level difference! And a matching power between two extremes without which our life would be impossible. Aquarius seems to deny his/her terrestrial origins, and Scorpio seems to forget his/her celestial ideal; one tends to go up and the other to go down.

It’s practically a relationship between two sun signs that most often communicate on completely different frequencies. A combination usually thought of as impossible or very difficult, as Aquarius isn’t very patient with Scorpio’s emotional, introvert and jealous nature, while Scorpio will hardly tolerate Aquarius’ careless independence.
